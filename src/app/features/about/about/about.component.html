Our Family Farm

We raise our cattle along the picturesque North Fork of the San Gabriel River in Liberty Hill, Texas. The majority of our land has been generously donated to The Nature Conservancy, and with a grant from the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department, we've established sustainable, rotationally grazed native grass fields. Our animals thrive in this spacious and clean environment, enjoying ample access to fresh air and sunshine. By prioritizing the well-being of our land and animals, we aim to provide our customers with the highest quality beef.

Exclusively raising registered Balancer cattle, we leverage 7 generations of lineage to craft our beef based on various genetic indicators, including maternal instinct, milk production, calving ease, and growth rates. Balancer cattle, a hybrid breed combining Gelbvieh and Angus, are known for their hybrid vigor, resulting in a higher growth rate and superior meat quality, as detailed on Wikipedia.

We take pride in our 'mamas,' who seem to think they are just big family pets. The Ridge at Loafer's Glory team comprises dedicated family members, veterans, and support staff who share a passion for what they do. Working diligently every day, we ensure our animals are well-cared for, and our crops are healthy and abundant.

Discover the Excellence: Loafers Glory Grass-Fed Beef Unveiled

Loafers Glory cattle have been exclusively grass-fed throughout their lifetime. The distinctive flavor of grass-fed beef is renowned for its enhanced minerality and a clean, robust 'beefiness.' Yet, the quality of grass-fed beef can vary significantly based on where and how it is raised. Our cattle graze on the abundant grasses of Central Texas, minimizing the need for extensive movement or energy expenditure to consume highly nutritious food. The result is exquisite marbling, rich minerality, and unparalleled deliciousness.

We firmly believe that the low-stress lifestyle our cattle enjoy directly contributes to the exceptional quality of our beef.
