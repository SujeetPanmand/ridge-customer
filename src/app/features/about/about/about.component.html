Our Family Farm

We raise our cattle on the north fork of the San Gabriel River in Liberty Hill, Texas. The bulk of our land has been donated to The Nature Conservancy and with a grant from Texas Parks and Wildlife Department we have established sustainable rotationally grazed native grass fields. Our animals are raised in this spacious and clean environment, with plenty of access to fresh air and sunshine. We believe that by taking care of our land and animals, we can provide our customers with the highest quality beef.

We only raise registered Balancers which allows us 7 generations of lineage to craft our beef based on multiple genetic indicators such as maternal instinct, milk production, calving ease and growth rates.

We take pride that our "mamas" think they are just big family pets. Our team at Ridge at Loafer's Glory is made up of dedicated family members and support staff who are passionate about what they do. We work hard every day to ensure that our animals are well-cared for and that our crops are healthy and abundant.
