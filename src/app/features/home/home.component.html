We are a veteran-owned family farm situated on the North Fork of the San Gabriel River in Liberty Hill, Texas. Our native grass-fed beef is known for its exceptional flavor and quality.

Why Native Grass-fed Beef ?

The majority of our land has been donated to The Nature Conservancy, and with a grant from the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department, we've developed sustainable rotationally grazed native grass fields. Our animals thrive in this spacious and clean environment, enjoying ample access to fresh air and sunshine. We firmly believe that by stewarding our land and caring for our animals, we can deliver the highest quality beef to our customers.

Certified facility, ensuring excellence from farm to table A beef quality grade is a comprehensive assessment of factors that impact the palatability of meat, including tenderness, juiciness, and flavor. These factors encompass carcass maturity, firmness, texture, color of lean, and the amount and distribution of marbling within the lean. Healthy Diet

Grass-fed beef is often considered to be healthier than grain-fed beef for a few reasons. Grass-fed beef typically has lower levels of unhealthy fats, such as omega-6 fatty acids, and higher levels of beneficial nutrients, such as omega-3 fatty acids and antioxidants. Additionally, grass-fed beef is usually leaner and contains higher levels of vitamins and minerals.
